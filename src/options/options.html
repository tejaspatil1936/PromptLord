PromptLord

PromptLord adds a one-click Enhance button to ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini that rewrites your prompt into a clearer, more detailed version for better AI responses.

Quick access: type a prompt, then click Enhance or press Ctrl + Shift + E (Cmd + Shift + E on Mac). PromptLord rewrites the text in place and never submits it for you.

No setup or API key is required — enhancement runs through PromptLord's free, secure backend.

Your prompts are processed transiently and are never stored. Read our privacy policy.
